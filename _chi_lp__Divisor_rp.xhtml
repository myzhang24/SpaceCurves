SpaceCurves :: chi(Divisor)

chi(Divisor) -- Euler characteristics of a Divisor

Synopsis

  • Function: chi (missing documentation)

Description

chi(Divisor) computes the Euler characteristic H^0(D)-H^1(D)+H^2(D) of an input divisor D using Riemann-Roch under the assumption that the surface D lies on is smooth.
