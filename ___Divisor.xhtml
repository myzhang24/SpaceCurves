SpaceCurves :: Divisor

Divisor -- Divisors on a PolarizedSurface

Description

Divisor is a type of HashTable encoding the information of a divisor on a PolarizedSurface.

The key Coordinate stores a List encoding the coordinates of the divisor with respect to the same basis as in the IntersectionPairing.

The key Surface stores the PolarizedSurface the divisor lies on.
