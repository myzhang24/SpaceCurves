SpaceCurves :: degree(Divisor)

degree(Divisor) -- Degree of a Divisor

Synopsis

  • Function: degree

Description

degree(Divisor) computes the degree of an input divisor D with respect to the polarization H. When D is effective, this gives the degree of the image curve under the rational map given by H.
