SpaceCurves :: Divisor + Divisor

Divisor + Divisor -- Operations on divisors

Synopsis

  • Operator: +

Description

One can multiply a Divisor by an integer or a rational number, as well as adding and subtracting two Divisors on the same PolarizedSurface.

Divisor * Divisor computes the intersection product of two Divisors on the same PolarizedSurface using the IntersectionPairing.
